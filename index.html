Introduction

Hello, I’m Boris. I’m a student in a research-oriented master degree in Computer Science at Université Paris Diderot (Paris 7). My aim is to understand the nature and the mechanism underlying computation and logic, and how they relate/separate human mind, nature and machines.

Interests

  • Linear Logic, Proof-nets, Geometry of Interaction, Ludics
  • New approaches in Computational Complexity
  • Curry-Howard Isomorphism and interpretations of Classical Logic
  • Lambda-calculi and Functional Programming
  • Type Theory and Interactive Proof Assistants
  • Quantum Computation and Quantum Programming
  • Dialogues, meaning and syntax in linguistics
  • Non-determinism in Logic and Computation
