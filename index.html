Introduction

Hello, I’m Boris. I’m a PhD student in Computer Science at Université de Villetaneuse Paris 13 (Team LoVe, LIPN). My aim is to understand the nature of Logic and Computation and their underlying mechanism. I would like to free knowledge from prejudices and biases as much as possible. I am sensitive to problems regarding education, critical thinking, beliefs, misinformation and the spreading of knowledge/information. I have the following dreams : to be able to speak over 10 languages, to travel around the world and to be able to clearly convey my thoughts and ideas especially by speaking (and also to be rich and famous). But I have to understand what I’m doing and studying in the first place.

Interests

  • Foundations of Logic and Computation
  • Linear Logic, Proof-nets, Geometry of Interaction, Ludics, Transcendental Syntax
  • Non-classical approaches in Computational Complexity
  • Minor interests: linguistics (syntax, semantics, pragmatics), quantum computation, interdisciplinary connections, social-psychological-biological understanding of logic, epistemology
